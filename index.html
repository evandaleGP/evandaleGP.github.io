About Us

We are located in the picturesque village of Evandale in Northern Tasmania, founded in 2023 by Vasuki and Darren. Prior to the founding of Evandale General Practice, Vasuki and Darren worked in a number of surgeries/hospitals around Tasmania allowing them to provide the highest level of medical excellence to the Evandale Community with a combined 30 years of experience between them.

GP Services

Regardless of your preferred language, we can help you to arrange an interpreter through TIS to assist with any language barriers during your consultation.
We provide video and phone consults and all walk in patients will be triaged and treated accordingly.

We provide a Full Range of GP Services
  • Chronic Medical Conditions [Eg Diabetes, Asthma, Heart Disease, Lung Disease]
  • Childhood Medicine and Vaccinations
  • Pregnancy Care
  • Preventative Medicine and Screening [eg Cervical Screening]
  • Minor Surgery
  • Acute illnesses and Injuries
  • Contraception and Sexual Health
  • Implanon insertion and removal
  • Mental Healthcare including Mental Health Care Planning
  • Workers’ Compensation
  • Travel Health and Travel Vaccinations
  • Pre-employment Medicals
  • Driver’s Licence Medicals [Commercial and Private Standards]
  • Health Assessments and Care Planning
  • Medical Termination of Pregnancy [MTOP]

Dr Vasuki Annamalai

MBBCh (Ireland) BAO FRACGP (Australia)

Dr Vasuki Annamalai was born in Jaffna, Sri Lanka and is of Tamil descent, with a rich family background in advocacy and education. Fluent in Tamil and English, she graduated from the National University of Ireland (Royal College of Surgeons) in 2004. Her extensive medical journey spans prestigious institutions across Singapore, New Zealand, and Australia, with particular expertise in obstetrics and general practice.

Director of Evandale General Practice, Dr Annamalai brings her diverse experience in both rural and urban healthcare settings to Tasmania. In 2021, she achieved Fellowship of the Royal Australian College of General Practitioners and Specialist registration with AHPRA, marking her commitment to excellence in primary care.

A fierce advocate for primary healthcare, Dr Annamalai is passionate about nurturing the next generation of medical practitioners. Beyond medicine, she is an accomplished powerlifter and outdoor enthusiast who embraces Tasmania's natural beauty through bushwalking. Her practice philosophy emphasizes work-life balance and comprehensive patient care, drawing from her rich international experience and deep understanding of diverse communities.

Her commitment to medical education and patient advocacy continues to shape the future of general practice in Tasmania, where she leads with both expertise and compassion.

COVID Requirements

For all Face-to-Face appointments please arrive as close as possible to your appointment time. 5 minutes prior to scheduled appointment would be appreciated. Regardless of your vaccination status; We ask you to NOT ENTER our practice if you:

  • Are waiting on your result for a Covid-19 test
  • Have cold or flu-like symptoms including a fever, cough, runny nose, sore throat, loss of taste and/or smell and shortness of breath. In this case on the day of your appointment we ask that at a minimum you return a negative RAT Test and that you wear a face mask prior to entering. Please ensure it covers your mouth and nose.

Telehealth is available to patients who are positive for covid 19/awaiting a result or have the above symptoms. A time slot will be booked with the GP and they will call you for a phone consultation at the allocated time. Practice staff will advise you if there will be an excessive delay in your appointment time. The Doctor will advise you if they assess you as requiring further Face-to-Face assessment. Our telehealth appointments follow the same fee structure as our Face-to-Face appointments. We do apologise, however due to Medicare rules If you have not been seen Face-to-Face at our practice previously or within the past 12 months you will not be eligible for a rebate from Medicare and your out-of-pocket cost will be 100% of our telehealth consult fee.
