About Us

We are located in the picturesque village of Evandale in Northern Tasmania, founded in 2023 by Vasuki and Darren. Prior to the founding of Evandale General Practice, Vasuki and Darren worked in a number of surgeries/hospitals around Tasmania allowing them to provide the highest level of medical excellence to the Evandale Community with a combined 30 years of experience between them.

GP Services

Regardless of your preferred language, we can help you to arrange an interpreter through TIS to assist with any language barriers during your consultation.
We provide video and phone consults and all walk in patients will be triaged and treated accordingly.

We provide a Full Range of GP Services
  • Chronic Medical Conditions [Eg Diabetes, Asthma, Heart Disease, Lung Disease]
  • Childhood Medicine and Vaccinations
  • Pregnancy Care
  • Preventative Medicine and Screening [eg Cervical Screening]
  • Minor Surgery
  • Acute illnesses and Injuries
  • Contraception and Sexual Health
  • Implanon insertion and removal
  • Mental Healthcare including Mental Health Care Planning
  • Workers’ Compensation
  • Travel Health and Travel Vaccinations
  • Pre-employment Medicals
  • Driver’s Licence Medicals [Commercial and Private Standards]
  • Health Assessments and Care Planning
  • Medical Termination of Pregnancy [MTOP]

Team Members

At Evandale General Practice we only hire the most qualified staff to ensure that every patient recieves the highest quality of care available. Our medical professionals are fully qualified to provide you with the best care available.

Dr Vasuki Annamalai

MBBCh (Ireland) BAO FRACGP

Dr Vasuki Annamalai was born in Jaffna, Sri Lanka and is of Tamil descent. Her Father was a Barrister and advocate for minority rights and her mother was a teacher. She is fluent in Tamil and English. She graduated from the National University of Ireland (Royal College of Surgeons) in Ireland in 2004, before Interning in Singapore. Working at Changi General Hospital, KK Women’s and Children’s Hospital, an 830 bed Major obstetrics, gynaecology and paediatric centre and Singapore General Hospital a Major tertiary referral hospital. Arriving in Australia in 2006 she worked at the Launceston General Hospital (LGH). From 2006 to 2008 Dr Annamalai worked within the Obstetrics team at the LGH. In 2008 she moved to New Zealand working ostensibly in Obstetrics and General Practice. In 2011 Dr Annamalai returned to Australia working in General Practice in the local Launceston area before returning to the LGH in the Emergency Department where she met her nurse husband to be, Darren. In 2013 she moved to Hobart working in the Stroke/Neurology team. In 2014 a return to General Practice in and around Launceston, marrying Darren in 2016. In 2017 Dr Annamalai and her Husband moved to Rural NSW, where she worked as a solo GP while also providing on call emergency and in patient care at the local hospital. In 2018 she moved to Tamworth NSW, working in General Practice in Werris Creek. Dr Annamalai has 2 children both born in Tamworth. At the end of 2020 Dr Annamalai and her family returned home to Tasmania with their 2 children. In 2021 she became a Fellow of the Royal Australian College of General Practitioners, obtaining her Specialist registration with AHPRA. She is an avid powerlifter, loves bushwalking, enjoys all the beauty Tasmania has to offer and loves Family Life where a balance is very important to her. A lesson learnt via her journey in Medicine. She is a fierce advocate for Primary care, her patients and nurturing the next generation of medical graduates towards a career in General Practice.

Darren Bowkett (RN)

Darren was born in Launceston and grew up in the Northern suburbs. He attended Rocherlea Primary School, Brooks High, Alanvale College and went on to complete his Bachelor of Nursing at the University of Tasmania. His Father was a Painter and Decorator and his Mother a Diversional Therapist. Darren worked as Registered Nurse at the Launceston General Hospital from 2007 to 2017 and again from 2021 to 2022. The Majority of that time was spent working in the Emergency Department. In 2012 he met Vasuki who was at the time a Registrar in the Emergency department. They went on to marry in 2016 and now have 2 beautiful children. Between 2017 and 2020 a move to the Mainland saw him working in Rural NSW, in towns such as Wee Waa (Cotton Capital of Australia), Narrabri, Tamworth (Country Music Capital) and Werris Creek. During that time working mainly in Emergency Departments (small and big) but a stint in General Practice working with Vasuki as her practice nurse. A stint that fired his interest in primary care which still remains. He returned to Tasmania in 2020 with his family. Since Late 2022 he has been working in Urgent Care, a role he has embraced and enjoyed. When time permits he loves to bush walk and really just be in nature. He loves to spend as much time with family and like Vasuki that balance between work and family life is very important. “Tasmania is home for me and that feeling is very strongly imbedded in me”.

COVID Requirements

For all Face-to-Face appointments please arrive as close as possible to your appointment time. 5 minutes prior to scheduled appointment would be appreciated. Regardless of your vaccination status; We ask you to NOT ENTER our practice if you:

  • Are waiting on your result for a Covid-19 test
  • Have cold or flu-like symptoms including a fever, cough, runny nose, sore throat, loss of taste and/or smell and shortness of breath. In this case on the day of your appointment we ask that at a minimum you return a negative RAT Test and that you wear a face mask prior to entering. Please ensure it covers your mouth and nose.

Telehealth is available to patients who are positive for covid 19/awaiting a result or have the above symptoms. A time slot will be booked with the GP and they will call you for a phone consultation at the allocated time. Practice staff will advise you if there will be an excessive delay in your appointment time. The Doctor will advise you if they assess you as requiring further Face-to-Face assessment. Our telehealth appointments follow the same fee structure as our Face-to-Face appointments. We do apologise, however due to Medicare rules If you have not been seen Face-to-Face at our practice previously or within the past 12 months you will not be eligible for a rebate from Medicare and your out-of-pocket cost will be 100% of our telehealth consult fee.
